ITB is located near the Junction of the M50 and the N3.
M50 Southbound
Travelling Southbound on the M50 take Exit 6 for Blanchardstown/ Navan/ Cavan (N3). Follow the slip road and take N3 towards Navan/Cavan.
Travel straight along N3 (through 2 sets of traffic lights) and take the second slip road for the Blanchardstown Town Centre.
At the top of the slip road, take a right (follow Institute of Technology road sign) and travel along this road to the next roundabout &
take the 1st exit which is the entrance to the ITB campus.
M50 Northbound
Travelling Northbound on the M50 take Exit 6 for Blanchardstown/ Navan/ Cavan (N3). Follow the slip road & take N3 towards Navan/Cavan.
Travel straight along N3 (through 2 sets of traffic lights) and take the second slip road for the Blanchardstown Town Centre.
At the top of the slip road, take a right (follow Institute of Technology road sign) and travel along this road to the next roundabout &
take the 1st exit which is the entrance to the ITB campus.
Travelling from the City Centre
Head for the N3 (main Navan/Cavan road). The first roundabout you will come to is at the Halfway House Pub (on the right hand side).
Go straight through the roundabout continuing on the N3. Travel straight along N3 through a set of traffic lights (Travelodge Hotel will
be on the right hand side). The next roundabout is the M50 roundabout. Again go directly through, continuing on the N3. Travel straight
along N3 (through 2 sets of traffic lights).
Take the second slip road for the Blanchardstown Town Centre. At the top of the slip road, take a right (follow Institute of Technology
road sign) and travel along this road to the next roundabout & take the 1st exit which is the entrance to the ITB campus.
Travelling from the direction of Navan/Cavan
Travelling along the N3 take the slip road for the Blanchardstown Town Centre. When you reach the top of the slip road, turn right.
Continue straight for a short distance and turn left (at the traffic lights) on to Blanchardstown Road North. Travel along this road for
approx. 1 mile to the next roundabout & take the 1st exit which is the entrance to the ITB campus.
